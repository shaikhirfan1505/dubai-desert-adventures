Frequently Asked Questions

What should I wear for the desert safari?
Comfortable clothes, closed shoes, sunglasses and light layers for the evening are recommended.
Are children allowed on quad bikes?
Yes — we offer kids' quad bike sessions with lower-powered machines and instructor supervision.
Is pickup included?
Most packages include pickup and drop-off from Dubai hotels and selected addresses. Please confirm when booking.
Do you provide helmets and safety gear?
Yes — helmets and necessary safety equipment are provided for quad and dune buggy activities.
Can I drive the dune buggy myself?
Yes — licensed drivers can drive our dune buggies after a short safety briefing. Some packages offer driven options by our guides.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the activity for most packages. Check terms during booking.
Are meals included?
BBQ dinner is included in evening safari packages; overnight packages include breakfast.
How long does the pickup take?
Pickup windows vary. We typically collect guests 45–90 minutes before the scheduled activity start time.
Is insurance provided?
Basic liability coverage is provided for guests; personal travel insurance is recommended for extra protection.
Can I book private or group packages?
Yes — we offer private and group bookings for events, celebrations and corporate outings.
